AWARD WINNING WEDDING PHOTOGRAPHY & FiLMS

Our passion for storytelling and attention to detail sets our wedding videography and photography apart while our friendly and easy going approach makes us just nice people to work with! Capturing real moments and real weddings for over 20 years we have seen all kinds of wedding days from every cultural and thematic background you can imagine. It's your unique personalities that we thrive on and bring out in the images we capture because everyone is different and everyone has a different story. Hi, we're Phil & Sam

As we are always behind the camera we rarely get in front it but here's one of those rare moments just so you can see who we are 🙂

We are Phil and Sam and we first actually met in 1994 at Castle School in Thornbury, South Gloucestershire where we spent our secondary school days annoying one another and a bit of studying and learning on the side. And naturally both of us having an interest in art and photography led us to where we are now.

Having known one another for such a long time is a massive selling point in what we do and makes our work the success it is because we simply get along and communicate so well. On a high-pressured live event this is invaluable. Time is short and moments are fleeting so being on the same wavelength and having the same creative goals is absolutely priceless.
